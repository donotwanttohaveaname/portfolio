What We Stand For

Our manifesto

Nine things we care about. Written down so we hold ourselves to them.

01

We choose ethical marketing

We believe in transparency, honesty, and professional responsibility. We want marketing that feels good to create and good to stand behind.

02

We support sustainable and ethical businesses

We care about long term growth, healthy teams, and practices that make the industry better for everyone.

03

We choose open collaboration

We show up to give as much as we receive. We keep conversations open and respectful, and we actually help each other out.

04

Fair pay is essential

All work deserves compensation. Commercial companies should never expect unpaid labour.

05

Recruitment should feel human

We aspire to keep communication clear, kind, and consistent. Compensation belongs openly in the job ad. No ghosting. Anyone who joins a process should feel valued from the first step.

06

We embrace immigration and diversity of backgrounds

We actively welcome and support immigrants entering the workforce. Many of us moved to Finland from somewhere else. We know what that's like, and we look out for each other.

07

Equal opportunities for everyone

Identity, gender, sexuality, ethnicity, religion, disability, or background should never limit access to opportunities.

08

A volunteer driven community

We are not a registered company, nor association. We are a home built by volunteers who offer their time and energy. Anyone can join for a project or for the long term, with no minimum commitment.

09

Zero tolerance for harassment

Everyone deserves to feel safe and respected here. No excuses. No exceptions.
