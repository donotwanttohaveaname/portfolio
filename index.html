05 · how i work

the approach, in four steps

01

define

messaging, audience, icp. who are we talking to, what problem do we solve, and why should anyone care. the boring-sounding step that decides everything.

02

build

funnel design from problem-aware to product-aware. lead magnets, content, ads, nurture sequences. the part where things actually get made.

03

distribute

find where buyers search and spend time. linkedin, google, email, cold outreach. pick the channels, launch the campaigns, reach actual humans.

04

improve

measure, iterate, experiment. influenced pipeline, channel metrics, what's working, what's not. rinse, repeat, get better. the step that never ends (in a good way).
